Google Chrome: Clearing your History, Cache and More.

Article ID: KB510004

Created: 1/1/2013

Overview

Sometimes it is useful to clear temporary files from your web browser. This can be done to solve an issue with logging into a specific site, display issues, or if a website is not loading properly. This article will teach you how to clear history from Google Chrome.

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Open Google Chrome and open the main menu located in the upper right hand corner with an ellipsis or hamburger menu.
  2. Choose History from the resulting menu.
  3. A list of your browser history will appear. Select Clear Browsing data...
  4. A small window will appear. Select the time range of history to delete. Please note that Bookmarks and shortcuts are not deleted in this process.
  5. Choose Clear Browsing Data after you've made your selections.
  6. You may need to close your browser and open it again for a display, login or rending issue to be completely resolved.
